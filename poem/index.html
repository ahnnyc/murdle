The Blue Note Affair

The rain came down hard on Delancey Street the night the Blue Note Jazz Club burned.

Fire marshals pulled the body of club owner “Lucky” Dolan from the wreckage at half past two. Faulty wiring, they said. Case closed. Except Lucky kept the real books — and someone needed those books gone.

The detective arrived before the investigators left. Scratched into the scorched brass nameplate above the door was a single word. Not a cry for help. A confession.

Can you crack the cipher in 6 guesses?
